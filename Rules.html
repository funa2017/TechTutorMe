RULES

For Students

We expect our students to act respectfully to both their teachers and their peers. Inappropriate behavior will not be tolerated as it creates a hostile environment for others. Also, remember that this is an educational site and not a platform for anything else.

For Teachers

We expect our teachers to act appropriately and accordingly when dealing with others. Please be mindful of your audience and remember that the purpose of our website it to help students. Remain calm, open minded, and flexible as you deal with students from all over the world.
